Intent

This website was designed to provide an interactive comparison of salaries, taxable benefits, and the employee reviews across Ontario public sector employers.
The intent is to highlight the differences and longitudinal dynamics between earnings and proportion of male and female employees as well as their roles in the organizations.


Of course, it would be most meaningful to view this kind of analysis on Ontario's making regular (below $100,000 annual) salaries. However and unfortunately, only salaries of the highest earners are available at the present time. The trends we see here are most likely indicative of the overall trends we would see for wider public sector employees.

Data Sources & Technology

Data presented on the dashboard was obtained from the Ministry of Finance (https://www.ontario.ca/page/public-sector-salary-disclosure).

Additional data was scraped from indeed.com (April 2019). Ratings information is static and will not be updated as ratings fluctuate over time. 

Technology used: Python programing language, Flask web micro-framework, Plotly Dash library, as well as multiple python libraries such as numpy, pandas, BeautifulSoup, etc. The conversion of names into genders was done with the help of gender_guesser (https://pypi.org/project/gender-guesser/)

All rights reserved. This dashboard is available for free use and reference. It is intended to be used as a general informational tool and should not be used to inform decision making at the employer level. For the official data source, please refer to the Ministry of Finance and indeed.com
